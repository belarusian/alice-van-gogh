Who was Van Gogh?

Vincent van Gogh was a super famous painter from the 1800s! He was born in the Netherlands and he painted SO many amazing pictures before he died at age 37.

💡 Fun fact: He only sold ONE painting when he was alive!!! Can you believe that?? But now his paintings are worth MILLIONS of dollars!

Things About His Life

🇳🇱 Born: March 30, 1853 in the Netherlands
🎨 Painted: Over 2,100 pieces of art in just 10 years!
🌻 Loved: Sunflowers, stars, nature, and bright colors
📝 Wrote: He wrote letters to his brother Theo almost every week

Fun Things I Learned! 🤓

  • 🎨 He didn't even start painting until he was 27!
  • 📧 He wrote over 800 letters in his life!
  • 🌻 He named a painting "Sunflowers" — pretty obvious lol
  • 🏠 He lived in a yellow house and painted it!
  • ⭐ He said the night sky is more alive than the day!
  • 💀 He died on July 29, 1890 when he was 37 — he shot himself, but he was really sad and sick. It's still a mystery what really happened!
